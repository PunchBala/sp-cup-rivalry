Mini Fantasy scoring
  • Fixture-only scoring: your 4-player team scores only from the fixture it locked against. There is no season carry-over into that match score.
  • Same MVP engine as Nerd Room / Duel: base player points are runs + (sixes x 2) + (wickets x 25) + (dot balls x 2) + (catches x 8) + (stumpings x 12).
  • Batting strike-rate bonus: applies once a batter either scores 30+ runs or faces 10+ balls, then SR above 170 = +8, above 150 = +5, above 130 = +2, below 100 = -5.
  • Bowling economy bonus: minimum 2 overs, then economy below 6 = +8, below 7 = +5, below 8 = +2, above 10 = -5.
  • Milestone bonuses: fifty +10, hundred +25, quick 30 under 15 balls +8, 3 wickets +12, 4 wickets +20, 5 wickets +30, and a duck penalty of -5 when a batter is dismissed for 0.
  • Mini Fantasy extras: each selected player who appears in the fixture gets +2. Each selected player from the winning side gets +5 once the result is final.
  • Captain + no-result rule: your captain gets 1.5x of every eligible point, including appearance and winner bonuses. If a fixture ends with no result, every Mini Fantasy player score for that match becomes 0.
  • Missed-lock safety net: if you miss a completed fixture after joining, you receive 40% of the average locked score for that fixture. Your first 3 missed locks are capped at 50 points each, then the cap drops to 30 from the 4th missed lock onward. If nobody locked a team for that fixture, nobody gets any fallback points.
  • Fixture-day visit bonus: signed-in users who open the app on an India fixture day receive +5 once for that calendar day in Asia/Kolkata.
  • New-player catch-up: if you join after Mini Fantasy has already launched, each completed earlier fixture starts you with 40 baseline points so you do not begin from 0.
  • Lineup total: add the four player totals exactly as shown on the locked team card. Those displayed player totals already include appearance bonus, winning-side bonus, and captain multiplier where applicable.
